## Project Overview
Bharat Sahayak is a voice-first, AI-powered assistant designed to help citizens easily access and understand government schemes using local languages.

## Functional Requirements
- Voice-based interaction for users
- Multilingual language support (Indian regional languages)
- Government scheme discovery and explanation
- Eligibility checking through guided questions
- Step-by-step application guidance
- Voice and text-based responses
- Low-bandwidth optimization

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Scalable serverless architecture
- High availability and reliability
- Secure access and data handling
- Fast response time
- Cost-efficient deployment

## Target Users
- Rural and semi-urban citizens
- Farmers, students, women, senior citizens
- Users with low digital literacy

## Constraints
- Internet connectivity may be limited
- Language diversity across regions
- Data accuracy and updates required

## Assumptions
- Government scheme data is publicly available
- Users have access to basic smartphones
